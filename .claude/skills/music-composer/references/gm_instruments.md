## Usage Tips

### Orchestral Writing
- Strings: 40-47, 48-49
- Woodwinds: 64-79
- Brass: 56-63
- Use realistic velocity and articulation

### Band/Rock
- Electric Guitar: 27, 29, 30
- Electric Bass: 33, 34
- Piano: 0-5
- Organ: 16-18

### Electronic/Synth
- Leads: 80-87
- Pads: 88-95
- Bass: 38-39
- Effects: 96-103

### Jazz
- Piano: 0, 1
- Bass: 32, 33
- Saxophone: 64-67
- Trumpet: 56
- Drums on channel 10

### Layering
Combine instruments for richer sound:
- Strings (48) + Choir (52)
- Synth Lead (81) + Synth Pad (88)
- Electric Piano (4) + Pad (89)
